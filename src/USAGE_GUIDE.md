## üéØ Feature Usage

### Chat Mode
1. Navigate to **Chat Mode** from home
2. Type your message in the input box
3. Click **Send** or press Enter
4. Receive instant grammar feedback
5. View corrections in sidebar
6. Click **Voice Input** to speak

### Roleplay 3D Mode (Complete Flow)

#### Step 1: Start Session
1. Navigate to **Roleplay Mode** from home
2. See pre-call screen with feature overview
3. Click **"Start Practice Session"**

#### Step 2: During Session
1. Avatar appears in center
2. Wait for AI to greet you (status: Speaking)
3. Click **Mic button** to start recording
4. Speak your response
5. Click **Mic button again** to stop
6. AI processes your speech (status: Processing)
7. View scores in left panel
8. Read transcript in right panel
9. AI responds (status: Speaking)

#### Step 3: View Quick Feedback
1. Click **"Show Feedback"** button at bottom
2. Modal shows:
   - Pronunciation score
   - Fluency score
   - Grammar score
   - Your speech transcript
   - Quick recommendations
3. Click **"View Full Summary & Save Result"**

#### Step 4: Result Summary Page
1. See overall score with emoji
2. Review individual scores with progress bars
3. Read full transcript
4. Check "Areas for Improvement"
5. Read AI recommendations
6. Choose action:
   - **Try Again**: Returns to Roleplay Mode
   - **Save Result**: Shows success modal

#### Step 5: Save & Continue
1. Click **"Save Result"**
2. See animated checkmark
3. Success message appears
4. Click **"Back to Dashboard"**
5. Result saved to Progress Page

### Progress Dashboard
1. Navigate to **Progress** from home
2. View stats cards at top
3. Scroll to see charts:
   - Line chart: Weekly improvement
   - Bar chart: Common errors
   - Pie chart: Skill breakdown
4. Track your learning journey

### Daily Challenge
1. Navigate to **Challenge** from home
2. Choose challenge type
3. Read question
4. Select/type answer
5. Click **Submit**
6. See immediate feedback
7. View final score
8. Check leaderboard position

### Settings
1. Navigate to **Settings** from top/bottom nav
2. Choose a tab:
   - **Profile**: Edit name, email, avatar
   - **Preferences**: Set learning goals, level
   - **Voice & Accent**: Choose accent, speed
   - **Notifications**: Toggle reminders
   - **Appearance**: Change theme
   - **Language**: Switch UI language
3. Make changes
4. Changes auto-save (toast confirms)

### Logout
1. Click **Logout** button
2. Confirmation modal appears
3. Avatar waves goodbye
4. Click **"Logout"** to confirm
5. Or click **"Cancel"** to stay
6. Success toast appears
7. Returns to Login Page

## üé® Visual Cues

- **Green**: Excellent performance (80-100)
- **Orange**: Room for improvement (60-79)
- **Red**: Needs practice (<60)
- **Blue**: Active/Selected state
- **Cyan Glow**: AI is listening
- **Purple Glow**: AI is processing

### Status Indicators
- **Green dot + "Connected"**: Session active
- **Pulsing animation**: Loading/Processing
- **Checkmark**: Success/Completed
- **Alert icon**: Needs attention

### Button States
- **Default**: Normal appearance
- **Hover**: Slight lift + shadow
- **Active**: Pressed down
- **Disabled**: Faded + no pointer

## üìä Understanding Your Scores

### Pronunciation (80-100)
- Clear phoneme articulation
- Correct stress patterns
- Natural intonation
- **Keep practicing!**

### Pronunciation (60-79)
- Some phoneme errors
- Inconsistent stress
- Work on specific sounds
- **Review feedback!**

### Fluency (80-100)
- Smooth speech flow
- Natural pace
- Good rhythm
- **Excellent work!**

### Fluency (60-79)
- Some hesitations
- Pace too fast/slow
- Practice more
- **You're improving!**

### Grammar (80-100)
- Correct structure
- Proper articles
- Right tenses
- **Outstanding!**

### Grammar (60-79)
- Minor mistakes
- Article issues
- Review rules
- **Keep going!**
